## Frequently Asked Questions

What type of support does Fable offer?
- We provide priority support in-app and via email. Our demo experts are always available for any questions you may have.

Are there any limits to creating new demos?
- There are no limits to creating/ editing demos in any of our plans.

Can I add audio & video annotations/ guides to a Fable demo?
- Yes, you very well can. In addition to text, you can add audio and video annotations to your Fable demo to make it as engaging as ever.

Are are any limits on demo viewers and demo views?
- There are no limits in demo views whatsoever in any of our plans.

What all URL parameters does Fable support?
- UTM parameters: to help you track exactly where a lead is coming in from. You'll be able to track all of these in our analytics dashboard. We not only pass the UTMs added to the demo URL but also the specific page where the demo was embedded.
- Email: If you'd like to keep a track of who's going through the demo without them completing a form, we can help you do that too.

What integrations does Fable support today?
- We natively integrate with SalesForce and HubSpot. We’re going live with Segment, Zapier, Marketo & Pardot very soon.

Will Fable help us in creating our first demo?
- Absolutely. We do provide 1:1 onboarding and implementation support to ensure your demos look super slick. Count on us and we’ll take you from a beginner to a power user of Fable in no time.
