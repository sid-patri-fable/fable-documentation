## Sharing Fable Demos: An Overview

Fable demos can be shared in various ways depending on your use-case. This page gives you an overview of the same. 

Once you’ve published your Fable demo, it is now ready to be shared. You can now let your audience interact with it in different ways: 

- **Embed the demo with our iFrame Embed**: You can embed a Fable demo on any landing page, blog, or a website using our simple iFrame Embed.
- **Share the demo via a URL**: Just copy the sharing URL of the demo you published and share it via email, chat, or anywhere else you’d like.
