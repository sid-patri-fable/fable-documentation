## Managing your Demos

This section will help you manage your demos. 

How to rename a demo? 

Click on the ⋮ menu of the demo you intend to rename. Click on “rename tour” and then “save” to make changes.
<img width="1104" alt="Screenshot 2024-02-21 at 9 19 18 PM" src="https://github.com/sid-patri-fable/fable-documentation/assets/155963795/e155a196-dfff-4b74-8b09-7357055ee9d4"></img>


How do I duplicate a demo?

Click on the ⋮ menu of the demo you intend to duplicate. Click on “duplicate tour” and then “save” to create a duplicate.
